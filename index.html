Our Story

We first met in college during a study abroad program in Italy. Michael was studying architecture and Sarah was studying art history. Our love blossomed over shared walks through ancient ruins and late-night conversations in tiny cafes.

After five wonderful years together, Michael proposed during a surprise picnic at the same spot where we had our first date. Of course, Sarah said yes!

Now we're excited to celebrate our love with all of our closest family and friends. We can't wait to see you all on our special day!
